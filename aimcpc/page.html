About

Aurora Internal Medicine Clinic, P.C. has been serving the southeast Aurora area since 1981. In 2004 we opened what is now our main office in Parker, Colorado. We also have a satellite location in Aurora, Colorado. Our locations utilize two different hospital systems to make access even easier. Our three doctors, certified physician’s assistant, Family Nurse Practitioner, and our excellent office staff work as a team to provide the best quality Internal Medicine care in our communities at a reasonable cost.

All our physician providers are board-certified and strive to keep up with the latest developments in their field. The three physicians all are involved actively in training new Internal Medicine physicians from the University of Colorado. In addition, they have participated in the training of both Nurse Practitioners and Pharmacists working towards their Pharm D. At the present time, we focus our care delivery in our two outpatient medical locations. Our goal is to provide evidence based healthcare and help empower our patients to effectively maintain their own health.

We look forward to working with you to keep you healthy and happy for many years to come.
